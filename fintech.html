FinTech Platform Design and Implementation

Traditional financial institutions’ approach to the customer service versus digital experience built by innovative fintech applications are certainly different. However, they both have one common goal: improving customer experience and building a relationship through a clear, personalised communication. We can help clients to revolutionise your fintech app by creating a unique customer experience with a personalised user interaction.

Celeric team over the last years have worked on several customised loan lending apps for our clients. Loan mobile apps is a disruptive business model which has filled a niche in the lending sector, with the aim of loaning money in an easy, flexible and trustworthy way. Lending applications today offer unique, conversion-driven user experience which is a key to success of any financial app.

According to our experience, following are the key drivers for a successful financial system development:





Customer experience

A well thought our design methodology is used to design product prototypes to test user adoption, experience, before any development process starts.

Security

Implement technologies secure financial systems through secure development methods, data, infrastructure and application security.

Scalability

Ensure that technology fits to business needs not the other way around. This way we ensure technology and infrastructure choice that is ready for feature application evolvement.
