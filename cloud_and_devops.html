Cloud Adoption & Migration

Organisations are adopting cloud very quickly. The biggest change in cloud transformation isn’t technology - it’s people. Successful cloud migrations require building unity and momentum behind a new, shared cloud-first vision. But human-centric transformation can’t be based on gut feelings. Technology decisions and migration planning require comprehensive discovery, accurate data, and fact-based road-mapping.

We take an application-centric approach to cloud management—building control, monitoring and automation into the parts of your business that drive the most value. Mature cloud management infuses business agility across the whole organization. In practice, the lead time between strategic intent and technology response all but disappears—you can develop faster, scale instantly and iterate constantly.

DevOps

DevOps Automation helps organisations continuously evolve and improve their applications by delivering changes at a faster pace. DevOps transformation is not just about the implementation of tools but it is a four-step process – Implementation, Measurement, Learning, and Improvement. Celeric helps organisations to develop a right strategy for DevOps and implement.

Agile is not just a methodology; it is a mindset. To kick-start your transition to DevOps, our team conducts a comprehensive analysis of client's requirements and the organisation to visualize the results. Our objective is to guide the clients through the transformation process and prepare the organisation with an intelligent automation and collaborative teamwork.
